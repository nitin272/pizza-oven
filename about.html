THE PIZZA OVEN

The Pizza Oven started as a small pizzeria in the streets of Delhi. Now, after 20 years of its inaugration, The Pizza Oven is India's biggest chain of fast food restaurants. The HQ of this affluent chain is located in the industrial complex of Gurugram in Ganga Vihar.
The Pizza Oven everyyear holds the foundation day on 11 August and celebrates the carnival in the Hq where food stalls are invited throught the world and chefs are invited to showcase their talent and the public loves to try exotic food from whole over the world.
